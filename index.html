Who I am

Hi, I'm Michael. I'm a software engineer, currently based in the Bay Area. I am a software engineer at Amazon and previously worked as an embedded software engineer at Aruba Networks. I'm a proud alumnus of Cal Poly SLO -- I graduated in 2021 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering. My specialization is in embedded systems and low-level software.
In high school, I was the lead software and electrical engineer on the Sea Sweepers ROV team, which placed second internationally at the college level.
I am always looking for opportunities in research or in industry for embedded software engineering and cryptography -- if you have any opportunities or questions for me, please do not hesitate to reach out.

Music Production
