Who I am

Hi, I'm Michael. I'm a fourth-year computer engineering student at Cal Poly SLO, who is currently looking for opportunities in research or in industry for embedded software engineering and cryptology. In high school, I was the lead software and electrical engineer on the Sea Sweepers ROV team, which placed second internationally at the college level. During the summer of 2019, I was an embedded software engineer at Aruba Networks, where I created and implemented a new API for their network switches' firmwares to use to more easily access line card data. I returned for a second internship at Aruba for summer 2020, and created a new mode for their network switches to allow hub-like functionality, decreasing the amount of old hardware the testing teams had to rely on.
